    System Privacy Policy

    Review our premium standard data privacy rules and global security compliance settings.

    1. Data Protection & Secure Storage

    All business operational database records, products lists, transaction logs, and customer contacts are completely isolated and stored securely. We use standard JWT signature validation to prevent any unauthorized cross-tenant requests.

    2. GDPR & CCPA Compliance Standards

    Leafy Dash respects user rights. You have the total ability to register locations, delete customer profiles, modify transaction histories, and update inventory counts dynamically. Deleting a user account cascades to erase all corresponding logs permanently.

    3. Zero External Information Sharing

    We maintain a strict zero-sharing policy. Your sales volume metrics, campaign discount coupon codes, VIP lists, and customer rating scores are never sent to outside analytics networks, remaining fully private to your store domain.
